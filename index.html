// Compliance First Architecture

Non-Negotiable Compliance.
Built Into Every Layer.

Every deployment ships with full statutory coverage. These are not optional modules — they are the foundation.

✓

KRA eTIMS Integration — VSCU/OSCU Certified

Automated invoice submission to KRA's eTIMS platform via VSCU and OSCU pathways. Every transaction is validated, signed, and transmitted in real-time. Zero manual tax filing for VAT-registered entities.

✓

M-Pesa Daraja API — Automated Reconciliation

Full Paybill and Buy Goods Till reconciliation against your Sage 200 ledger. Incoming and outgoing M-Pesa transactions are matched, categorized, and posted without human intervention.

✓

Statutory Payroll — NSSF, SHA & Housing Levy

Automated computation of NSSF contributions (new rates), NHIF/SHA premiums, and the 1.5% Affordable Housing Levy. Generates compliant pay slips and statutory returns each cycle.

✓

Data Sovereignty — Kenya-Hosted Infrastructure

All financial data resides on Kenyan-hosted servers, in full compliance with the Kenya Data Protection Act (2019). No sensitive records are processed outside Kenyan jurisdiction.
